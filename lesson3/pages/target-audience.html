Target Audience

People at least 18 years old of any race and gender looking to start and grow a business, or to prevent one already owned from going bankrupt.

Personas

Persona: Animal and Grain Farmer
Fictional Name: Ol' McDonald
Job Title: CEO of EIEIO Farms
Demographics:
70 years old
Married
Has PhD in Animal Science
Goals and Tasks: Focused and goal-oriented when it comes to his farms.
His main concern is making sure he has enough healthy
feed for all of his animals. Spends his time reviewing
tractor routes and the health of his animals.
Environment: He is not very comfortable using the internet, so one
of his farmhands helps him whenever he needs. He is
connected through a 5G network with a decent computer.
He uses the internet mainly to review farm
related purchases.
Quote: "I didn't have a farm, I still do! How can I market better
to the young ones?"
Persona: Unemployed Entrepreneur
Fictional Name: Steve Without a Job
Job Title: Prospect Entrepreneur
Demographics:
21 years old
Single
Has a Bachelors of Science on Business
Goals and Tasks: Has worked hard on the patent of a tech device, and is
working hard to establish a business where he can sell
his product to the world. His main concern is the bureaucracy
and the legal details of starting a business.
Environment: Very comfortable using the internet. Has access to high-speed
internet 24/7. Mainly uses his mobile phone
to connect to the internet and do his business and legal research
to start his business.
Quote: "My tech is the future. You'll see."

Scenarios

  • Can I get a loan to start a business? How do I apply for one?
  • My business went bankrupt, how can I get back on my feet?
  • I am planning a date. Where is the biggest outside mall?
  • How do I pay corporate tax?
  • Where can I find feed suppliers for my aquarium?
